Event Highlights: Free Event – Donations will be collected for the American Cancer Society. Ages: 12 and under. Nut-Free Event – Please ensure no food containing nuts. Kids dressed as Santa Claus hand out candy from a trunk filled with Christmas decorations, complete with twinkling lights and holiday cheer. Fun fact: Even Santa loves Halloween at Trunk or Treat!

Little mad scientists in lab coats and goggles share colorful gelatin treats from their science lab-themed trunk, filled with bubbling beakers and fun experiments. Fun fact: These “scientists” whip up sweet, spooky treats with a twist!

Step right up to the circus-themed trunk, with kids dressed as clowns and ringmasters. It’s all big top fun with candy and carnival-style games! Fun fact: The greatest show on wheels comes right to the parking lot!

It’s playtime at the Toy Story-themed trunk! Meet characters like Buzz and Woody, while candy and toys are shared in this colorful, nostalgic setup. Fun fact: To infinity and beyond – your candy haul is waiting!

Roar your way to the dinosaur-themed trunk, where kids dressed as prehistoric creatures hand out dino-sized candy! Fun fact: This trunk is a Jurassic journey back in time with sweets as the treasure!
